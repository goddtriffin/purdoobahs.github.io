What should I bring to band camp?

Bring a good pair of gym shoes. You will marching for almost every waking moment once you get to camp, so don't only bring Converse and your Timbs. You will also want a good water bottle and lots of sunscreen. I know we are situated in Indiana, but it will definitely still get HOT.
